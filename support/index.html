Frequently Asked Questions

Why do unfinished tasks carry over to the next day?

Karm is designed around the idea that important tasks shouldn't be forgotten. Any task you don't complete is automatically moved to the next day so nothing slips through the cracks.

How do I set an alarm for a task?

You can set an alarm while creating a task or editing an existing one. When creating or editing a task, use the clock icon that appears to choose an alarm time.

How do I delete a task?

Tap on a task to see its options, then select Delete Task.

My widget isn't updating. What should I do?

Open the app once to trigger a widget refresh. If the issue persists, try removing and re-adding the widget from your home screen.

How do I turn off ads?

Ads help keep Karm free. There is no option to disable them at this time.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. Karm has no accounts or cloud sync. To preserve your data, use your device's standard backup (iCloud or Google One).
